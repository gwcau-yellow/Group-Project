Sea Pollution

    Americans use about 500 million plastic straws each day.
    182.5 billion straws are used each year.
    8 million tons of plastic waste end up in the ocean each year
    Plastic items can take up to 1000 years to decompose in landfills.
    It takes about 500 years for plastic straws to decompose.
    Plastic bags take about 10- 1000 years to decompose.
    Plastic bottles take about 450 years to decompose.
    About 100,000 marine animals die of plastic waste each year.
    Garbage in the ocean comes trash from trash cans and landfills from streets and houses.
    80% of the sea pollution comes from land.
