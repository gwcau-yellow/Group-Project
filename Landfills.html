Landfills

Facts about landfills:

Americans can throw away 4.4 to 5 pounds of trash a day.

Two thirds of landfills cause harmful gases such as Methane, C02, etc.

About one third of an average dump is made of packing material.

The United States is the #1 trash-producing country, with 1,609lbs per person, each year.
